    • "People Watching"

    In the midst of the 2023 Level Her Up Game Jam, Goya played a key role in bringing "People Watching" to life. Inspired by the simple yet captivating act of observing café life, she delved into the world of C# coding using Unity, creating a narrative-driven game with the help of Yarn Spinner.
    Beyond the coding challenges, Goya also contributed to the game's atmospheric music, adding a personal touch to the project. The game's charm lies in its humble roots—an exploration of people watching in a café, with players gently influencing the characters' conversations.
    The collective effort bore fruit as "People Watching" found a place in the Level Her Up stand at the Perth Games Festival 2023. This modest achievement reflects not just the team's ability to meet the Game Jam's 48-hour deadline under the theme of 'connection,' but also our journey in developing and applying new skills to create an experience resonating with a wider audience.
